Title: British Grand Prix staying at Silverstone until 2034 | RACER
The British Grand Prix will stay at Silverstone until 2034 with a 10-year contract extension.
Silverstone, hosting F1 races since 1950, retains its historical significance.
F1 CEO Domenicali values the venue's history and praises the BRDC for their dedication.
The extension allows for further investments and developments at Silverstone.
