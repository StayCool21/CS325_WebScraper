Title: Bearman credits support from Sainz and Vettel with boosting his F1 debut | RACER
Ollie Bearman praised Sebastian Vettel and Carlos Sainz for their support during his F1 debut at the Saudi Arabian Grand Prix.
Bearman, filling in for Sainz, narrowly missed Q3 and finished seventh.
He received encouraging messages from Vettel and F1 CEO Stefano Domenicali, and guidance from Sainz and Charles Leclerc.
Bearman found the F1 weekend intense but rewarding, especially the physical challenge.
