Title: IMSA attracting growing interest from prospective new entrants | RACER
IMSA's Mobil 1 Twelve Hours of Sebring event showcased a surge in interest from potential new entrants.
The absence of the FIA World Endurance Championship allowed for expanded participation across various IMSA categories.
Team owners from diverse racing backgrounds explored joining IMSA, with positive feedback from President John Doonan on the series' growth and appeal to manufacturers and fans.
