Title: Not sold on Formula E? Dario Franchitti thinks you need to reconsider | RACER
Formula E, despite initial skepticism, has evolved into a competitive and respected racing series.
Dario Franchitti, a former IndyCar driver and Formula E commentator, emphasizes the excitement and challenges of the all-electric championship.
He highlights the series' progress, dispels misconceptions, and praises its innovation and sustainability efforts, urging fans to give it a chance.
