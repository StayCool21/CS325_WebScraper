Title: VCARB 01 breaks cover in Las Vegas | RACER
The VCARB 01, the first car under the new Visa Cash App RB name, was unveiled in Las Vegas.
The former AlphaTauri team rebranded as Racing Bulls with Visa and Cash App as title partners.
The car features elements of Toro Rosso colors with new white and red additions.
The team launch highlighted key personnel and limited technical details.
Daniel Ricciardo emphasized the importance of sponsors in supporting the team's progress.
Formerly AlphaTauri, the team finished eighth in the previous season's constructors' championship.
