Title: Ocon confirms ongoing Mercedes links ahead of 2025 vacancy | RACER
Esteban Ocon remains connected to Mercedes amid Lewis Hamilton's move to Ferrari for 2025.
Ocon, managed by Mercedes, aims to perform well with Alpine to be considered for the Mercedes seat.
Hamilton's switch has stirred early driver market activity, creating uncertainty.
Teammate Pierre Gasly sees Hamilton's move as beneficial for F1.
